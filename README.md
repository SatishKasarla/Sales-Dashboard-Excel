# Sales-Dashboard-Excel

🚚 SwiftGo Sales Dashboard | Sales Performance & Business Insights

A dynamic, interactive Microsoft Excel Dashboard built to analyze sales performance across multiple dimensions—focusing on revenue, order trends, customer ratings, food category performance, regional sales distribution, and business KPIs to support data-driven decision-making.

2. Short Description / Purpose

The SwiftGo Sales Dashboard is a visually engaging and analytical Microsoft Excel Dashboard designed to help businesses monitor and evaluate their sales performance. The dashboard highlights key business metrics such as total sales, order volume, customer ratings, average order value, regional sales distribution, and food category performance.

This dashboard is intended for Business Owners, Sales Managers, Operations Teams, Marketing Professionals, and Data Analysts who want to track sales performance, identify business trends, and improve operational efficiency through data-driven insights.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Microsoft Excel – Main platform used to build the interactive sales dashboard.
📂 Power Query (Excel) – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
📈 Pivot Tables – Used to summarize and analyze sales data efficiently.
📊 Pivot Charts – Used to create dynamic and interactive visualizations.
🎛️ Slicers & Timeline Filters – Used for interactive filtering by Month, Category, and Restaurant.
🧮 Excel Formulas – Used for KPI calculations, aggregations, lookup functions, and business logic.
🎨 Conditional Formatting – Used to enhance data visualization and highlight key metrics.
📁 File Format – .xlsx for dashboard development and .png for dashboard preview.

5. Features / Highlights
• Business Problem

Food delivery businesses generate thousands of transactions every day. Without an interactive dashboard, it becomes difficult to monitor sales performance, identify top-performing locations, evaluate customer satisfaction, and understand category-wise sales trends.

Key questions such as:

Which states generate the highest sales?
What is the total sales revenue?
Which food category contributes the most revenue?
Which cities are the top-performing markets?
How do daily and monthly sales fluctuate?
What is the average customer rating?
Which quarter performs the best?
How many orders are placed across different periods?

...are difficult to answer quickly using raw sales data.

• Goal of the Dashboard

To deliver an interactive sales analytics dashboard that:

Enables businesses to monitor overall sales performance.
Tracks order volume and revenue trends.
Analyzes customer ratings and average order values.
Identifies top-performing food categories, cities, and states.
Supports strategic business decisions using interactive reports.
Helps management optimize sales performance and operational planning.

• Business Impact & Insights

Sales Performance Monitoring: Enables management to track revenue, orders, and customer ratings through a centralized dashboard.

Regional Sales Analysis: Helps identify top-performing states and cities for targeted marketing and expansion strategies.

Category Performance: Provides insights into the sales contribution of different food categories, supporting inventory and menu optimization.

Customer Experience: Tracks customer ratings and average order value to evaluate customer satisfaction and service quality.

Strategic Decision Making: Interactive reports allow business leaders to analyze trends, monitor KPIs, and make informed operational decisions.
